The arrondissement of Toulouse is an arrondissement of France in the Haute-Garonne department in the Occitanie region. It has 225 communes. Its population is 1,049,948 (2016), and its area is 2,538.9 km2 (980.3 sq mi).

== History ==
The arrondissement of Toulouse was created in 1800.
